Building Bonds, One Paw at a Time

At Paw and Heart, we're more than just a pet adoption service . We're a community united by love and compassion for animals. Our mission is simple yet profound: to provide homeless pets with a second chance at life and connect them with loving families who will cherish them forever.

Who we are

Founded by passionate animal lovers, paw and heart was created to tackle the growing need for pet rescues and adoptions. With countless pets seeking homes every year, we work tirelessly to ensure each animal in our care is safe, healthy, and prepared for a loving future.

What we do

  • Rescue & Rehabilitate: We partner with shelters and rescue organizations to save pets from neglect, abandonment, and overpopulation.
  • Prepare for Adoption: Every animal receives proper medical care, vaccinations, and socialization before they're ready to meet their forever family.
  • Support Families: Adoption is just the beginning of a lifelong bond. We offer resources, advice, and training tips to help families provide the best care for their new companions.
